About

I'm a UX Designer actually based in Milan. Once completed my bachelor's degree I decided to improve my knowledges about Design and in particular the ways it is correlated with the digital medium. For this reason I attended a Master in Web and Digital Design, where I better understood what user experience and usability are. Finding my passion on these fields, it's been two years that I made them my profession.

During my work experience I understood how testing is essential in every phase of the design process. I focused a lot on it, practising and studying about the theme. In the last two years I had the chance to draw up and facilitate some usability tests, even with an eye tracker tool, when needed.

Furthermore I like to spend some time hiking and also cooking, experimenting new receipts.
