Dolce & Gabbana App

UI and UX Design

Dolce & Gabbana Application designed at Scuola Politecnica di Design during the Art Direction Strategy for Luxury and Made in Italy classes. The main purpose was to improve the customer experience inside D&G Maisons using technology. To design the project I used SketchApp and Invision then.

The application is created for both sales assistants and customers. Once registered, the Customer will receive, if requested, notificatins and invitations about exclusive events, runaways show, special collections and personal suggestions about products. Easy access will be provided with a small amount of personal details or QR code given in-store during registration.

The sales assistant will have the capability to register new customers, find easily the loyal ones and know different details about them before/meanwhile they're in store. It could be a way to enhance the luxury experience and the customer's expectations about it.
