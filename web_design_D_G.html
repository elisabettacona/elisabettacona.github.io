Dolce & Gabbana App

UI and UX Design

Dolce & Gabbana Application designed at Scuola Politecnica di Design during the Art Direction Strategy for Luxury and Made in Italy classes. The main purpose was to improve the customer experience inside D&G Maisons using technology. To design the project I used SketchApp and Invision then.
