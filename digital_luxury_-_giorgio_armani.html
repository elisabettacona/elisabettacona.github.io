Designed and Prototyped with Axure®

Create an omnichannel Experience for the Made to Order section to enrich overall Armani shopping experience online was the client's brief. The aim was to translate, in the digital environment, the benefits and the expertise a customer receives in store while keeping the online experience as luxurious as in the stores.

Digital Luxury

Giorgio Armani

Made to Order is the new Giorgio Armani feature that allows client to receive a personalized suit in 2 weeks, using combinable standard sizes.


The starting point was the research, that involved some benchmark and an interview with the made to measure expert at Giorgio Armani store.

Based on the results three personas, with related customer journeys, were created.


Once the main process was defined, some wireframes were created and then user interface design completed the overall.

In the last step the customer can personalize his suit changing fabric, lining and buttons based on the previous choices. Here the client can interact with the interface using 360 degree model of the final suit.

User can also combine different sizes of jacket and trousers.


Desktop and Mobile prototypes are available.

The final output was a guided Online Experience divided into 4 steps, while trying to maintain the experience as luxurious as possible, intuitive and innovative.


The first three steps try to recall the instore experience with some helpful questions that will restrict the number of fabrics and styles suggested in the fourth step.

Designed at Scuola Politecnica di Design.
