ABAcedario

Editorial Graphic

ABAcedario is a particular project created at Accademia di Belle Arti di Catania during the Graphic Design classes. It's a little monography of the C letter created around the topic of the Land Art and the following sections: Name, Thing, City, Color, Artist, Font. The picture in the cover create the shape of the letter C.
