Agape Adoption

Service Design - UI and UX Design

During the Service Design Workshop at Scuola Politecnica di Design we choose a topic, adoption in this case, and we try to improve some aspect of that. So we focus on some kind of activities, like babysitting, volunteer at the orphanage, meetings to help parents to stay in touch and share stories of adoption. The final project was Agape, a website for future parents that helps them to adopt a child and spend time with children while waiting during the long adoption process. SketchApp was used to design the website and InVision to create the prototype available here .
