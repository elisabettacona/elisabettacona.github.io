Agape Adoption

Service Design - UI and UX Design

During the Service Design Workshop at Scuola Politecnica di Design we choose a topic, adoption in this case, and we tried to improve some aspect of that. So we focused on some kind of activities, like babysitting, volunteer at the orphanage, meetings to help parents to stay in touch and share stories of adoption. SketchApp was used to design the website and InVision to create the prototype available here .

The final project was Agape, a website for future parents that helps them to adopt a child and spend time with children while waiting during the long adoption process. Below the system map of the main purpose of the website: activities.
