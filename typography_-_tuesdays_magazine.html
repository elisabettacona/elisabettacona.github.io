About

Tuesdays is a fashion digital magazine, with a strong focus on typography, that talks to a young audience who loves fashion and consumes it through smartphones and web.

For the musthead of this magazine I used a font linked with the fashion typographic world, but also young and elegant, Italiana. The typeface can be related to the Bodonian style, but in a modern and fresh Sans Serif version.


I used all uppercase characters and played with the U and A of the word TUESDAYS, in order to make the shapes of the word more consistent and I also modified the Y, making it more clean. Then I increased the tracking.

The magazine was also extended on the web enviroment, with a responsive website. To design the project I used InDesign, SketchApp and also Photoshop and AfterEffect to create animations and gifs.

I also chose two other typefaces to work with the one of the musthead: Playfair Display for headlines and Futura for the body.

The former is consistent with the elegant personality of the magazine, it has beautiful shapes at big sizes and is has different weights. The latter is clean sans serif in contrast with the serif Playfair Display, it's easy to read and it has a huge family.
