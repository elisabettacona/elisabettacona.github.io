Tuesdays Magazine

Digital Typography - UI and UX Design

Digital magazine designed at Scuola Politecnica di Design during the Digital Typography classes.
Tuesdays is a fashion digital magazine, with a strong focus on typography, that talks to a young audience who loves fashion and consumes it through smartphones and web.

For this magazine I wanted a font linked with the fashion typographic world, that is also young and elegant, so I chose Italiana, a typeface related to the Bodonian style, but in a modern and fresh Sans Serif version. I also chose two typefaces to work with the one of the musthead: Playfair Display for headlines and Futura for the body.

The magazine was also extended on the web enviroment, with a responsive website. To design the project I used mainly InDesign, SketchApp and also Photoshop and AfterEffect to create animations and gifs.
