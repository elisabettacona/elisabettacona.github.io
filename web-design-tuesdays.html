Tuesdays Magazine

Digital Typography - UI&UX Design

Digital magazine designed at Scuola Politecnica di Design during the Digital Typography classes. Tuesdays is a fashion digital magazine and talks to a young audience who loves fashion and consumes it through smartphones and the web. The magazine was also extended on the web enviroment, with a responsive website. To design the project I used mainly InDesign, SketchApp and also Photoshop and AfterEffect to create animations and gifs.
