Giorgio Armani Made to Order

UX and UI Design - Art Direction

Create an Omnichannel Experience for the Made to Order section to enrich overall Armani shopping experience online was the brief the client gave to my team. With this starting point in mind we tried to translate, in the digital environment, the benefits and the expertise a customer receives in store and keeping the online experience as luxurious as in the stores.
If you want to take a look, Desktop and Mobile Invision prototype are available.

Made to Order is a new feature Giorgio Armani provides to his customers. Choosing it you can have a personalized suit in 2 weeks, with standard sizes that you can combine.

From the Made to Order Armani section on the website you are also able to choose between Book an Appointment or the Online Experience to create your own suit.

The main focus is the Online Experience to create a personalized suit. We divided this luxury experience in 4 steps, trying to maintain it as luxurious as possible, intuitive and innovative.

In the first step you can choose between the two main style: Business or Ceremony.

In the second step you can choose between three different fit: Slim, Soft or Classic.

In the third step you can choose between the two collections: Summer or Winter.

Last but not least, in the fourth step you can personalize your suit. You can change the fabric, the lining and the buttons. You are also able to combine different sizes between jacket and trousers.
