The Seed of Art is a campaign focused on Land Art. The brief of this project was to create a campaign showcasing the 3 brand pillars that sustain the commitment to nature of the brand, part of the “Enrich not Exploit” brand statement to be communicated across different channels, on and offline.


The Idea was to promote eco-friendly Body Shop lifestyle, art and cultures connected to the ingredients used buy the company to create its products and, with this in mind, build an awareness about environmental risks for children's health.

The campaign focuses on the word “SEED” and its relation with the key points: ingredients as seeds of nature, land artists as seeds of art and kids as seed of future.

14 key ingredients by The Body Shop, 14 different countries and 14 different artists were involved. The campaign would take place in trade communities, where ingredients come from. Last but not least, trade communities' children would be involved and would participate to create the final piece of art.

All these activities would be leaded by 14 various artists, guided by local person from TheBody Shop. They would discover a foreign culture while living new adventures.


"The Seed of Art" campaign would be developed and entirely described in a Microsite linked to the current e-commerce through banners. The project would follow a timeline, but artists would be selected by users with contests.

Artists would spend one month in the country selected, taking a digital diary of their experience. People would follow the experience on social media like Instagram, Facebook, Twitter and YouTube through contents generated by artists and The Body Shop.

Both would use the campaign's hashtags: #TheSeedOfArt #SeedingBeautyBack #LandArtExperience #KidsSeedsOfFuture.

The campaign will be supported by two type of magazines: Fashion magazine, with a focus on the beauty products, and Travel Magazine, focused on contemporary art, children's cause and culture.

In stores, key ingredients' sections will be dedicated to the campaign. The packaging also, will be involved. At the end a book will be print, to collect all the pieces of art created during the campaign by land artists and children.
