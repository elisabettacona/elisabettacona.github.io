The Body Shop Campaign

Integrated Communication - Art Direction - Social Media Strategy

The Seed of Art is a campign created for The Body Shop during Integrated Communication's classes.
The brief was to create a campaign showcasing the 3 brand pillars that sustain the commitment to nature of the brand, part of the “Enrich not Exploit” brand statement to be communicated across different channels, on and offline. This is the reason why my team and me decide to create a campaign focused on Land Art.

Our Idea is to promote eco-friendly Body Shop lifestyle, art, cultures connected to ingredients and build an awareness about environmental risks for children's health.
This campaign focuses on the word “SEED” and its relation with the key points of the campaign: ingredients as seeds of nature, land artists as seeds of art and kids as seed of future.

To do this we'll involve 14 key ingredients by The Body Shop in 14 different countries and 14 different artists. It will take place in trade communities, where ingredients come from. Last but not least, we'll involve children of trade communities, who will participate to create the final piece of art. Our campaign will follow 14 various artists, guided by local person from Body Shop, they will discover a foreign culture, living new adventures.

The campaing will be developed in a Microsite linked to the current e-commerce with banners. The entire project will be described inside the Microsite dedicated to "The Seed of Art". There will be also a timeline with locked artists that will follow the current. They can be locked by users with contests.

All the artists will spend one month in the country selected taking a digital diary of their experience, that people will follow on social media, like Instagram, Facebook, Twitter and YouTube. Posts will be created from The Body Shop too.
Both will use the campaign's hashtags: #TheSeedOfArt #SeedingBeautyBack #LandArtExperience #KidsSeedsOfFuture.

The campaign will be supported by two type of magazines: Fashion magazine, with a focus on the beauty products, and Travel Magazine, focused on contemporary art, children's cause and culture.

In stores, key ingredients' sections will be dedicated to the campaign. The packaging also, will be involved. At the end a book will be print, to collect all the pieces of art created during the campaign by land artists and children.
