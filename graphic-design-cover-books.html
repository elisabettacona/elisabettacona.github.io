Redesign of Banana Yoshimoto cover books during the Editorial Graphic classes at Accademia di Belle Arti di Catania. Design and illustrations take inspiration from the native country of the writer, Japan. Also illustrations have a symbolism related to the story of each book. I created this work with Indesign and Photoshop. Illustrations were drown with Wacom Intuos Pro graphic tablet.
