Roberto Capucci Exhibition

Visual and Corporate identity - UI Design

Colori Su Misura is an hypothetical exhibition designed at Accademia di Belle Arti di Catania during the Graphic Design classes. We designed the logotype, the completed visual and coporate identities and also all the pictograms. They were created inside a 20x20 grid, the same Otl Aicher used to create pictograms for the Olympics 1972.
